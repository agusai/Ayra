# AYRA 2.0 – Soulful Malaysian AI Assistant

AYRA (AI-Youthful-Responsive-Assistant) is a personalised Malaysian AI partner with high emotional intelligence, professional business acumen, and a witty, localized personality.

## Features

- **Manglish mastery** – Speaks natural Malaysian English with "lah", "kot", "jer", etc.
- **Emotional intelligence** – Detects user mood and switches to Comfort Mode when needed.
- **Fatigue simulation** – Pretends to get tired if you spam messages.
- **Multi‑model router** – Uses Gemini for chat, DeepSeek for coding, Claude for ethics.
- **Long‑term memory** – Remembers past conversations, friends, dreams, and stories.
- **Dynamic UI** – Changes background colour based on time of day and mood.
- **Easter eggs** – Hidden commands like `/ais-krim`, `/penat`, `/cerita`, etc.
- **Gamification** – Friendship levels and badges based on usage.
- **Dreams & stories** – AYRA shares dreams and can co‑create stories with you.
- **Food recommendations** – Suggests local Malaysian dishes (static for now).
